Market Trends

Socio-economic trends set to emerge in a post-COVID society

Should we be optimistic?

Whether it’s shifting to online, expanding into a new market, or catering to a new kind of conscious consumer – businesses will have to rethink the way they run in a post-COVID society.

Shift to a home-centric and a digital-first world

Organizations realizing quickly that human behaviors post COVID-19 will pivot to ‘cocooning’ will gain trust sooner.

Covid-19 will impact digital transformation

The online channel which has been a discretionary one for the brick and mortar companies so far will become one of the mandatory channels in future.

Data analytics & insights led interventions will increase

From how countries are fighting the pandemic to organizations monitoring staff productivity, there will be more interest to tap the power of technology.

The acceleration of existing business trends

Things like consolidation of big tech and the death of traditional media. More stress in small & medium enterprises, more restructuring , more M&As and more Bankruptcies.

A growing sense of anxiety

Rapid penetration of insurances, bio-research, and in anything to do with healthcare. We have seen the power of “anxiety economy” in promoting wellbeing gadgets, and gadgets that monitor air quality etc

The Wealth Divide

The ability to spend and consume is now non-existent for a large section of population.

Innovation

What worked in the past, won’t work going forward. This is an opportunity for innovation.
